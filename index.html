Our Services

Miner & East has been appraising, reconstructing, and consulting on buildings damaged by insurance losses for more than 125 years. Our unique blend of expertise in insurance loss, reconstruction and, new construction gives us a comprehensive understanding of every stage of building restoration and development.

Construction Consulting

Miner & East has a long-standing reputation in the construction consulting industry. We have provided consulting on thousands of insurance loss projects nationwide, including major claims from fire, hurricanes, tornadoes, floods, and other catastrophic events. Our understanding of the insurance industry ensures accurate, reliable guidance and efficient recovery processes for every client.

Insurance Loss Reconstruction & Emergency Services

Miner & East is a true general contracting firm with in-house staff of estimators, project managers, superintendents, carpenters, clerical, and accounting personnel. We understand what it takes to stabilize and protect a building following an insurance loss. With extensive experience in claim preparation and emergency reconstruction, our team has successfully restored a wide variety of buildings across our long history.

New Construction & Renovations

Over the decades, Miner & East has completed a wide range of new construction and renovation projects for commercial, industrial, and retail properties. Our experience as general contractors, combined with our expertise in reconstruction, positions us to deliver quality craftsmanship across every assignment.
